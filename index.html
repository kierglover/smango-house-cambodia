Location

SMango House is located along the Mekong River in Prek Bongkong Village, Svay Chroum District, Kandal Province, 1.7 KM north of Wat Svay Chroum (and the white elephant in the intersection).

Take a boat up the Mekong from Phnom Penh, or cross on the Arey Ksat or the Svay Chroum ferry (car, motorcycle, bicycle). Then, continue 5.4 KM north from the Arey Ksat Ferry or 1.7 KM north from the Svay Chroum Ferry.
